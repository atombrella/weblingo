Bruno Bozzetto is known for many cartoons, primarily Signor Rossi, and infamous
for characterizing Italians as quite diverse in many ways in a small cartoon.

link to Europe vs. Italy

Most of his work was made in the 1970s and 1980s, and also gained popularity
outside of the country, e.g., the Signor Rossi series is popular in Germany.
